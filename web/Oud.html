The Oud

History  -  Diversity  -  Craftmanship

The Oud is a traditional musical instrument that is used in Middle Eastern music. The melodies and songs that it plays symbolize the cultural legacy and traditional history
of its diverse people.  
Its form is a strong symbol of Middle Eastern craftmanship that is used as a tool to tell the stories and the emotions of the history that it comes from.
