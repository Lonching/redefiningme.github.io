The Watermelon

Hospitality  -  Growth  -  Nurturing

While the watermelon holds a distinct modern political interpretation, the roots of the watermelon, run deep in Middle Eastern culture. It symbolizes growth and nurture.  
Grown across the Middle East, the watermelon has always been a fruit that  connects. Beyond its sweet taste, the watermelon embodies unity, hospitality, and the forever enduring spirit of the region.
