The Olive

Peace  -  Continuity  -  Heritage

The olive is an international symbol of peace and tranquility, but it holds a much deeper meaning in how it is seen in Middle Eastern culture. The olive symbolizes industry and generational culture. Olive trees, standing resilient through time, are not just agricultural producers of the fruit, but are a testament to familial legacies and ancesteral tradition.  
The olives collected from the trees are a symbol of continuity,  
connecting the present to the long-standing heritage of the Middle Eastern people.
