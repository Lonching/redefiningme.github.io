The Waw

Community  -  Expansion  -  Connection

The 27th letter in the traditional Arabic alphabet, the letter “waw” has a stand alone meaning. which directly translates into the word “and.” It stands as a symbol of community and togetherness.  
More than just a linguistic connecter, this letter embodies the potential to be more. The Middle Eastern culture and people are more than the stereotypes that limit them  
and the narratives that are placed upon them.
