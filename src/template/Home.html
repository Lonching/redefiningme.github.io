Redefining Representation is a campaign devised to remind the community of the symbolism
that connects and embodies the Middle Eastern culture. Crafted as a response to defy the harmful
narratives and stereotypes that the Middle Eastern people have more recently been subjected to,
Redefining Representation aims to elevate the positives of the community, traditions, culture, and
heritage of the Middle East through symbolic representation.
